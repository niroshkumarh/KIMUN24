Kumaraguru Institutions, Coimbatore, with a legacy of 40 years of educational excellence, encompasses academic institutions and centres of excellence in the domains of engineering, technology, management, science, agriculture, innovation, entrepreneurship, liberal arts and humanities, aspiring to offer distinct world-class education. Situated in a sprawling 156-acre campus in the IT corridor of Coimbatore, the institution maintains high standards of education and equips the students with knowledge, skills and competencies which empower them to effectively contribute to the economic and societal development of the nation.
